# SSC CHSL Exam Pattern

## Overview

SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level) examination is conducted to recruit candidates for Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant, and Data Entry Operator (DEO) posts in various ministries/departments of Government of India.

## Tier-wise Exam Pattern

### Tier I (Preliminary Examination)

| Subject | Questions | Marks | Time | Negative Marking |
|---------|-----------|-------|------|------------------|
| English Language | 25 | 50 | 60 minutes | 0.50 marks |
| General Intelligence | 25 | 50 | (Total time) | 0.50 marks |
| Quantitative Aptitude | 25 | 50 | 60 minutes | 0.50 marks |
| General Awareness | 25 | 50 | (for all 4 sections) | 0.50 marks |
| **Total** | **100** | **200** | **60 minutes** | **0.50 marks per wrong answer** |

**Mode:** Computer Based Test (CBT)
**Language:** Hindi and English (except English Language section)
**Qualification:** Must score above cut-off marks to qualify for Tier II

### Tier II (Main Examination)

| Component | Marks | Time | Mode |
|-----------|-------|------|------|
| Essay Writing | 60 | 60 minutes | Pen and Paper |
| Letter/Application Writing | 40 | (Total time) | Pen and Paper |
| **Total** | **100** | **60 minutes** | **Offline** |

**Language:** Hindi or English (candidate's choice)
**Nature:** Qualifying (minimum 33% marks required)
**Topics:**
- Essays on current affairs, social issues, economic matters
- Formal letters, applications, and reports

### Tier III (Skill Test/Typing Test)

#### For LDC/JSA Posts - Typing Test

**English Typing:**
- **Speed Required:** 35 words per minute
- **Duration:** 10 minutes
- **Error Allowance:** Maximum 5% errors

**Hindi Typing (Devanagari):**
- **Speed Required:** 30 words per minute  
- **Duration:** 10 minutes
- **Error Allowance:** Maximum 5% errors

**Note:** Candidates can choose either English or Hindi typing

#### For DEO Posts - Data Entry Speed Test

**Data Entry Test:**
- **Speed Required:** 2000 key depressions per hour
- **Duration:** 15 minutes
- **Error Allowance:** Maximum 5% errors
- **Mode:** Computer-based

#### For Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant Posts - Typing Test

**English Typing:**
- **Speed Required:** 35 words per minute
- **Duration:** 10 minutes
- **Error Allowance:** Maximum 5% errors

## Detailed Marking Scheme

### Tier I Scoring
- **Total Questions:** 100
- **Total Marks:** 200
- **Correct Answer:** +2 marks
- **Wrong Answer:** -0.50 marks
- **Unattempted:** 0 marks

### Subject-wise Distribution in Tier I
| Subject | Weightage | Difficulty Level |
|---------|-----------|------------------|
| English Language | 25% | Moderate to High |
| General Intelligence | 25% | Moderate |
| Quantitative Aptitude | 25% | Moderate |
| General Awareness | 25% | Moderate |

### Final Merit Calculation
**Final Merit = Tier I Marks Only**

*Note: Tier II and Tier III are qualifying in nature and do not contribute to final merit ranking*

## Important Instructions

### General Guidelines
1. **Normalization:** Marks are normalized if exam conducted in multiple shifts
2. **Calculator:** Not allowed in any tier
3. **Rough Paper:** Provided during CBT
4. **ID Proof:** Valid photo ID mandatory
5. **Admit Card:** Must carry downloaded admit card

### Tier I Specific
- **Sectional Time Limit:** No sectional timing
- **Question Paper:** Available in Hindi and English
- **Navigation:** Can move between questions freely
- **Final Submission:** Must submit before time ends

### Tier II Specific
- **Writing Material:** Blue/black ball point pen only
- **Answer Sheet:** OMR sheet provided
- **Word Limit:** Approximately 200-250 words for essay
- **Handwriting:** Should be legible
- **Language Consistency:** Stick to chosen language throughout

### Tier III Specific
- **Practice Time:** Usually 5-10 minutes practice time given
- **Typing Material:** Passage will be provided
- **Accuracy:** Focus on accuracy over speed
- **Keyboard Layout:** QWERTY keyboard layout

## Posts and Departments

### Available Posts
1. **Lower Division Clerk (LDC)** - Various Ministries/Departments
2. **Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA)** - Various Ministries/Departments  
3. **Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant** - Department of Posts
4. **Data Entry Operator (DEO)** - Various Ministries/Departments

### Salary and Benefits
- **Pay Scale:** Level 2 (₹19,900 - ₹63,200)
- **In-hand Salary:** ₹18,000 - ₹22,000 (approximately)
- **Allowances:** DA, HRA, TA as per government rules
- **Other Benefits:** Medical facilities, LTC, pension schemes

## Preparation Strategy

### Time Allocation (for 60 minutes)
- **English Language:** 12-15 minutes
- **General Intelligence:** 12-15 minutes  
- **Quantitative Aptitude:** 15-18 minutes
- **General Awareness:** 10-12 minutes
- **Review:** 5-8 minutes

### Section-wise Tips
1. **English:** Focus on reading comprehension and grammar
2. **Reasoning:** Practice regularly to improve speed
3. **Quantitative Aptitude:** Master basic arithmetic and algebra
4. **General Awareness:** Stay updated with current affairs

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Time Management:** Don't spend too much time on difficult questions
2. **Negative Marking:** Avoid random guessing
3. **Question Selection:** Attempt known questions first
4. **Panic:** Stay calm during the examination
5. **Technical Issues:** Report any technical problems immediately
