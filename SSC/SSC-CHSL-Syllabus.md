# SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level Examination) Syllabus

## Examination Structure

The SSC CHSL examination is conducted in 3 tiers:
- **Tier I**: Computer Based Examination (CBE) - Preliminary
- **Tier II**: Descriptive Paper in English/Hindi
- **Tier III**: Skill Test/Typing Test

## Eligibility

- **Educational Qualification**: 12th Pass or equivalent from a recognized Board
- **Age Limit**: 18-27 years (relaxation as per government rules)

## Tier II Syllabus (Descriptive Paper)

**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Maximum Marks:** 100 marks
**Mode:** Pen and Paper mode
**Language:** Hindi or English

### Essay Writing (60 marks)
- Topics may be related to current affairs, social issues, economic issues, environmental issues
- Word limit: approximately 200-250 words

### Letter/Application Writing (40 marks)
- Formal Letters
- Official Letters
- Applications
- Reports

**Topics for Essays and Letters:**
- Social and Economic Issues
- Environmental Concerns
- Government Policies
- Current Affairs
- General Topics of National Interest

## Tier III (Skill Test/Typing Test)

### For Data Entry Operator (DEO) Posts:
**Data Entry Speed Test**
- Speed Required: 2000 key depressions per hour
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Error Percentage: Maximum 5% errors allowed
- Mode: Computer-based

### For Lower Division Clerk (LDC) and Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA) Posts:
**Typing Test**

**English Typing:**
- Speed Required: 35 words per minute
- Duration: 10 minutes

**Hindi Typing:**
- Speed Required: 30 words per minute
- Duration: 10 minutes

**Note:** Candidates can choose either English or Hindi typing test

## Important Notes

1. **Negative Marking:** 0.50 marks deducted for each wrong answer in Tier I
2. **Qualifying Nature:** Tier II and Tier III are qualifying in nature
3. **Final Merit:** Based on marks obtained in Tier I only
4. **Normalization:** Applied if exam conducted in multiple shifts
5. **Language:** Tier I available in Hindi and English (except English Language paper)

## Posts Available

- **Lower Division Clerk (LDC)**
- **Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA)**
- **Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant**
- **Data Entry Operator (DEO)**

## Preparation Tips

1. **Create a Study Plan:** Allocate time for each subject based on your strengths and weaknesses
2. **Practice Mock Tests:** Regular practice helps improve speed and accuracy
3. **Current Affairs:** Read newspapers and magazines regularly
4. **Previous Year Papers:** Solve last 5-10 years' papers
5. **Time Management:** Practice completing the test within the given time
6. **Typing Practice:** For Tier III, practice typing regularly to achieve required speed
