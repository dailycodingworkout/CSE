# Important Dates for SSC Examinations

## SSC Annual Calendar

SSC follows an annual examination calendar which is usually released in April/May each year. Below are the typical examination schedules:

### SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level Examination)

| Phase | Activity | Tentative Timeline |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Notification** | Release of official notification | February - March |
| **Application** | Online application submission | February - March (3-4 weeks) |
| **Correction** | Online application correction window | Usually 2-3 days after application ends |
| **Admit Card** | Tier I admit card release | July - August |
| **Tier I Exam** | Computer Based Test (CBT) | August - September |
| **Tier I Result** | Result declaration | October - November |
| **Tier II Admit Card** | Tier II admit card release | November - December |
| **Tier II Exam** | Computer Based Test (CBT) | November - December |
| **Tier III Exam** | Descriptive paper | December - January |
| **Tier IV** | Skill test/Computer proficiency test | January - February |
| **Final Result** | Final result and merit list | March - April |

### SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level Examination)

| Phase | Activity | Tentative Timeline |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Notification** | Release of official notification | November - December |
| **Application** | Online application submission | November - December (3-4 weeks) |
| **Correction** | Online application correction window | Usually 2-3 days after application ends |
| **Admit Card** | Tier I admit card release | May - June |
| **Tier I Exam** | Computer Based Test (CBT) | June - July |
| **Tier I Result** | Result declaration | August - September |
| **Tier II Exam** | Descriptive paper | September - October |
| **Tier III** | Skill test/Typing test | October - November |
| **Final Result** | Final result and merit list | December - January |

### SSC JE (Junior Engineer Examination)

| Phase | Activity | Tentative Timeline |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Notification** | Release of official notification | September - October |
| **Application** | Online application submission | September - October (3-4 weeks) |
| **Correction** | Online application correction window | Usually 2-3 days after application ends |

| **Paper I Result** | Result declaration | May - June |
| **Paper II Exam** | Descriptive paper (Pen & Paper) | June - July |

## Other SSC Examinations

### SSC GD (General Duty) Constable
- **Notification**: Usually in July-August
- **Application**: August-September
- **Exam**: November-December
- **Result**: February-March (next year)

### SSC CPO (Central Police Organisation)
- **Notification**: Usually in March-April
- **Application**: April-May
- **Paper I**: June-July
- **Paper II**: August-September
- **Result**: October-November

### SSC Stenographer
- **Notification**: Usually released every 2-3 years
- **Application**: As per notification
- **CBT**: Usually 4-5 months after application
- **Skill Test**: 2-3 months after CBT result

## Important Reminders

### Key Points to Remember:
1. **Tentative Dates**: The above dates are tentative and may change
2. **Official Calendar**: Always refer to the official SSC calendar for exact dates
3. **Regional Variations**: Some dates may vary by region
4. **Holiday Adjustments**: Dates may be adjusted due to national holidays
5. **Multiple Shifts**: Exams may be conducted in multiple shifts over several days

### Preparation Timeline Recommendations:

#### For SSC CGL:
- **12 months before**: Start comprehensive preparation
- **6 months before**: Intensify preparation and mock tests
- **3 months before**: Focus on revision and speed improvement
- **1 month before**: Final revision and practice papers only

#### For SSC CHSL:
- **8-10 months before**: Start preparation (considering 12th level syllabus)
- **4 months before**: Regular mock tests and practice
- **2 months before**: Focus on weak areas and speed
- **2 weeks before**: Light revision only

#### For SSC JE:
- **12-15 months before**: Start with engineering concepts
- **8 months before**: Complete syllabus coverage
- **4 months before**: Practice and mock tests
- **1 month before**: Final revision of formulas and concepts

## Document Preparation Timeline

### During Application Period:
- **Photograph**: Recent passport-size photo (digital format)
- **Signature**: Digital signature
- **Certificates**: Scanned copies of educational certificates
- **Category Certificates**: If applicable

### After Selection:
- **Original Documents**: Keep all original certificates ready
- **Photocopies**: Make multiple sets of attested photocopies
- **Passport**: Apply for passport if planning for Central Government jobs
- **Medical Examination**: May be required for certain posts
