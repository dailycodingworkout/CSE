## ðŸ† Exam-Specific Weightage

### GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering)
- **Total Marks:** 15 marks (10 questions)
- **General Aptitude Section:** 5 Verbal Questions
- **Key Focus:** Grammar, Vocabulary, Reading Comprehension, Sentence Completion
- **Difficulty Level:** Moderate to High (tricky options)

### ESE (Engineering Services Examination)
- **Paper-I:** General Studies & Engineering Aptitude
- **English Section:** 15-20% weightage
- **Key Focus:** Precise grammar, formal vocabulary, comprehension

### PSU Exams
- **Varies by Organization:** 10-25 questions typical
- **Key Focus:** Business communication, formal writing skills

### Bank Exams (IBPS, SBI, RBI)
- **Prelims:** 30 English questions (30 marks)
- **Mains:** 35-40 questions
- **Key Focus:** Error spotting, Cloze test, Para-jumbles, RC
