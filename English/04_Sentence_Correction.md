## 1. Subject-Verb Agreement Errors

### The Core Rule
```

```

### Common Traps

#### Trap 1.1: Words Between Subject and Verb

```

```
âŒ The CEO, along with his team, ARE attending.

```

#### Trap 1.5: "The number" vs "A number"

## 14. Conditional Errors

### The Three Main Conditionals

```

## 15. Miscellaneous Errors

### Trap 15.1: Less vs Fewer

```
FEWER = Countable nouns (discrete items)
"Fewer books, fewer people, fewer mistakes"

LESS = Uncountable nouns (mass)
"Less water, less time, less money"
```

### Trap 15.2: Much vs Many

```
MANY = Countable: many books, many people
MUCH = Uncountable: much water, much information
```

### Trap 15.3: Farther vs Further

```
FARTHER = Physical distance
"The airport is farther than I thought."

FURTHER = Abstract/metaphorical distance
"Further discussion is needed."
"Let's take this matter further."
```
