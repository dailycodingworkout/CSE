# Understanding Idiomatic Expressions

## ğŸ§  The Mental Model: Idioms as Compressed Stories

```
An idiom is a PHRASE whose meaning is NOT the sum of its parts.

LITERAL: "He kicked the bucket" = He struck a bucket with his foot
IDIOMATIC: "He kicked the bucket" = He died

You can't DECODE idioms - you must KNOW them.
```

# Phrasal Verbs

## ğŸ§  The Mental Model: Verb + Particle = New Meaning

```
VERB + PREPOSITION/ADVERB = PHRASAL VERB (often different meaning)

"Give" = to provide
"Give up" = to surrender/quit
"Give in" = to yield
"Give away" = to donate/reveal
```
