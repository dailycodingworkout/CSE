## 1.1 What is Spatial Aptitude?

### Definition
Spatial aptitude is your brain's ability to:
1. **Perceive** visual information accurately
2. **Rotate** objects mentally in 2D/3D space
3. **Transform** shapes through operations (fold, cut, reflect)
4. **Reconstruct** objects from partial or transformed views
